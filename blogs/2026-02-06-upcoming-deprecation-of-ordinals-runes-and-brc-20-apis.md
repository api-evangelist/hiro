Hiro will be deprecating its Layer-1 Bitcoin tooling, including support for Ordinals, Runes, BRC-20, and Bitcoin Indexer. We’ve worked with Xverse to create an easy transition path for developers to their API’s for these use cases. This post covers what’s changing, when it’s happening, and how to migrate with minimal friction.
