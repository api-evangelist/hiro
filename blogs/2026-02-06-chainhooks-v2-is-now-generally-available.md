We're excited to announce that Chainhooks v2 is now generally available and officially out of beta. This release represents a significant step forward in reliability, performance, and developer experience. As we transition to v2, we will be deprecating the hosted version of Chainhooks v1 entirely on March 1st, 2026. This post covers what's new in v2, what's changing, and how to migrate from v1.
